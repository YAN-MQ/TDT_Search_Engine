New Zealand Prime Minister Jenny Shipley will make her first official 
visit to Singapore Nov. 19-20, Singapore's Foreign Affairs Ministry 
said Tuesday. Shipley will confer with her Singapore counterpart, 
Goh Chok Tong, and Deputy Prime Minister Tony Tan. She also is scheduled 
to attend briefings at the Ministry of Trade and Industry, witness 
Singapore Airlines operations, speak to the business community, and 
pay her respects to New Zealand soldiers killed in Singapore in World 
War II.

Shares of Daimler-Chrysler are losing 1 1/2. Today is the first day 
of trading for the new company formed by the merger of Chrysler and 
Germany's Daimler-Benz, maker of Mercedes Benz. News reports say the 
newest auto giant will build a beefier full-size pickup truck to compete 
against the like of Ford's F-series super duty line. And Daimler-Chrysler 
is reportedly also eyeing the Asian market with a Low-priced four-door 
sedan. The price tag, about $7,000. No word on when these vehicles 
would roll off assembly lines.

LINZ, Austria (AP) -- Fire heavily damaged a major warehouse and distribution 
center of the appliance company Technik-Quelle, and officials Sunday 
estimated losses at 200 million schillings, or about dlrs 17 million. 
The cause of the fire, which was extinguished Sunday after 17 hours, 
was undetermined, according to the Austria Press Agency. But the blaze 
severely damaged the main warehouse and distribution center for the 
chain's Austrian operation of the German-owned company. About 80 firemen 
and 23 firetrucks battled the blaze in the city's Danube River harbor 
area. The firm retails household supplies and appliances.

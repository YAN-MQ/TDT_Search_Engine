Separatist militants and security forces fought a day long gunbattle 
in India's northern Kashmir state Sunday, leaving four militants and 
two army soldiers dead. A defense spokesman said the fighting took 
place in a village north of Kashmir's summer capital, Srinagar. In

Kashmir. Unidentified men also attacked and damaged a power transmission 
center near Srinagar, the spokesman said.

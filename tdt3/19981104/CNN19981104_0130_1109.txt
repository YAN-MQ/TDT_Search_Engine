British airways is sprucing up its long-haul economy class. Tuesday, 
the airline announced a $150 million effort to upgrade the world traveler 
cabins in its fleet of long-haul jets. Among the improvements, personal 
video screens for each passenger, roomier seats and self-service snack 
bars. Special features for business travelers will also be added including 
a new home check-in service by telephone.

Chancellor Gerhard Schroeder will travel to Moscow on Nov. 16-17 for 
talks with President Boris Yeltsin, his spokesman said Wednesday. 
The German leader, who formally took over from former Chancellor Helmut 
Kohl last week, will also meet Prime Minister Yevgeny Primakov, spokesman 
Uwe-Karsten Heye said. Topics are expected to include economic reforms 
in Russia and bilateral matters, he said.

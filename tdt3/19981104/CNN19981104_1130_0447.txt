Voters in many states also had the chance to decide ballot initiatives. 
Michigan voters overwhelmingly defeated a proposition to legalize 
doctor assisted suicide. Allowing medical use of marijuana was on 
the ballot in several states. Voters in Alaska, Arizona, Nevada and 
Washington state passed the measure. Results still are incomplete 
in Oregon, but it appears the measure will pass. Voters in Washington

the second state to ban racial or gender preferences in government 
hiring and contracting or in college admissions.

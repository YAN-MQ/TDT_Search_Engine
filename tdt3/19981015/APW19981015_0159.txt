Russian police arrested 65 drug dealers in one day, the Interior Ministry 
said Thursday. The dealers arrested Wednesday had in their possession 
953.8 grams (33.6 ounces) of heroine, 1.283 grams (less than half 
an ounce) of opium, 27.5 kilograms (60.6 pounds) of marijuana, and 
more than 10 kilograms (22 pounds) of poppies, the ministry's press 
office told the ITAR-Tass news agency. The arrests took place throughout 
the country, it said.

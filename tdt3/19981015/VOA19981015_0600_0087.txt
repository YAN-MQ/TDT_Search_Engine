The Lebanese Parliament has elected popular General Emlie Lawann as 
the countries next President. All of the one hundred legislatures 
were present at the session voted for the General who is supported 
by Syria. He is to be sworn in for a six-year term and on November 
24th the day President Elias Herrari leaves office. The General was 
selected for the post last week at a Damascus meeting. Syria, which 
dominates Lebanese politics, has more than 30,000 troops in the neighboring 
country.

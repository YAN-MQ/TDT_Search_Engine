An Israeli woman was stabbed and lightly wounded Thursday by a masked 
man who fled the scene in a car, Israeli radios reported. The stabbing 
took place outside a small rural community in southern Israel, the 
radios said. The woman, who was not immediately identified, was taken 
to a hospital in the nearby city of Ashkelon for treatment. Israeli 
radios said police were mounting a massive search for the vehicle.

Sumitomo Electric Industries of Japan says that plans to set up a 
joint venture cable television company with Toshiba Corporation in 
January. Sumitomo Electric, a leading maker of electric wires and 
cables said the venture is expected to start operations in April. 
It will provide cable television, related services to domestic users, 
including consulting and engineering. Sumitomo Electric will hold 
a 55 percent stake in the venture and Toshiba will own 45 percent.

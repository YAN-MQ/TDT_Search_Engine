A Jamaican prosecutor will return to Grenada to serve for two years 
as a legal advisor to the government and the police force, officials 
here announced Friday. Hugh Wildman, 37, deputy director of the Office 
of the Director of Public Prosecutions, had previously worked in Grenada 
1994 and 1995 as a prosecutor. As part of his duties he will oversee 
investigations, work with police to ensure that sufficient evidence 
is collected for prosecutions and will conduct training sessions.

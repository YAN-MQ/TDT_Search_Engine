That ubiquitous yellow Smiley face from the '70s is coming back on 
a Postage stamp. Thursday the postal service announced it and 14 other 
winners in its celebrate the century series that commemorates each 
decade. Americans voted for their favorites at post offices and over 
the Internet. The Smiley face beat out "Sesame street" for the number 
one symbol of the '70s by about 100,000 votes. They were followed 
by earth day, disco and VCRs. Other winners include the nation's bicentennial, 
Monday night football, the pittsburgh steelers, jumbo jets and medical 
imaging. The women's rights movement, '70s fashions, the pioneer 10 
spacecraft, racehorse secretariat, and TV's "all in the family."

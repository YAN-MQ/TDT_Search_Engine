The House Judiciary Committee is scheduled to meet this week to debate 
possible articles of impeachment for President Clinton. Hearings begin 
on Tuesday at 9 a.m. The cable news networks _ CNN, Fox News Channel, 
C-Span and MSNBC _ plan continuous live coverage of the hearings and 
any votes on articles of impeachment. C-Span will rebroadcast the 
proceedings at 7 p.m. Court TV also intends to cover the hearings 
and any vote on articles of impeachment. ABC, CBS and NBC will have 
live coverage of any votes on articles of impeachment.

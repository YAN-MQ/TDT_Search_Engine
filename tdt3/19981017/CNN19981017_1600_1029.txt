Third-quarter reports were rosy for two of the big three automakers. 
Massive cost-cutting continues to pay off for Ford, which registered 
a $1 billion profit for the third quarter. Chrysler set an earnings 
record with $682 million in third-quarter profits. Only General Motors, 
devastated by the summer's two parts plant strikes, reported a third-quarter 
loss -- $809 million.

Two buses collided in southern Brazil on Saturday, killing 17 people 
and injuring 48, police said. The accident occurred when a bus taking 
tourists to the Iguacu waterfalls near Brazil's border with Argentina 
and Paraguay collided with another bus heading in the opposite direction, 
said Parana state police Sgt. Carlos Vieira. The accident occured 
near the town of Candoi, 420 miles (680 kms) southwest of Rio de Janeiro. 
Police were investigating the cause.

Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has appointed Aerial Sheron 
as foreign minister. Mr. Sheron will also take charge of Israel's 
negotiations with the Palestinians. The appointment becomes days before 
a summit in Washington with President Clinton and Palestinian leader 
Yasser Arafat. From Jerusalem, LEE TUSET reports. Seventy year old 
Aerial Sheron takes over this portfolio from Mr. Netanyahu himself 
and will now sit beside the prime minister during this crucial stage 
of peace talks. Palestinians and left wing Israelis have denounced 
the promotion of this prominent hard-liner a champion Jewish settlements 
in the occupied West Bank. But Mr. Netanyahu won't conclude any peace 
deals unless he carries many of his right-wing allies with him. The 
man to deliver them is Aerial Sheron, a retired general and former 
defense Minister, security is at the top of his agenda. Lee Tuset, 
BBC, Jerusalem.

guerrillas who killed nearly two million Cambodians, surrendered to

at Preah Vihear temple near the border with Thailand. ``We ask for 
permission from the Royal Government of Cambodia to rejoin society 
and the Royal Cambodian Armed Forces,'' Khem Nuon said in Friday's 
surrender statement, broadcast Saturday. The Khmer Rouge laying down

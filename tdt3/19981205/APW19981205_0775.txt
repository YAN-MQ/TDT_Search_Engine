Four German tourists died and 48 others were injured Saturday after 
their bus slid off a snowpacked dirt road and landed in a ravine. 
The Austria Press Agency reported that the accident occurred after 
the driver pulled off a main road near Linz, about 160 kilometers 
(100 miles) west of Vienna,and onto a dirt road to put snow chains 
on the vehicle. The bus began sliding after it was stopped and then 
dropped eight meters (yards) into a ravine. Some of the injured were 
in serious condition, said the agency. The bus was carrying 70 people, 
including the driver.

The number of tourists arriving in San Juan by cruise ships increased 
by 15.6 percent for the 1997-98 fiscal year, showing the continued 
growth in the popularity of cruises, officials said Saturday. According 
to statistics from the Ports Authority, 1,273,011 cruise ship passengers 
passed through San Juan's port this fiscal year. The Authority predicts 
that the total will top 2 million per year by 2010.

A Texas death row inmate remains on the loose. Authorities say 29-year-old 
Martin gurule scaled two razor-topped fences thursday to get out of 
the Ellis one prison unit. The facility is 80 miles north of Houston.

restaurant in 1992. It's the first time a condemned killer has escaped 
from a Texas prison in 64 years.

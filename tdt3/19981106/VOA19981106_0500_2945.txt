Stock prices on the major exchanges in Europe were up fairly sharply 
this Friday in Zurich, Paris, Frankfurt, and in London. The eight 
major indices are all posting solid gains. Stock prices down moderately 
in Tokyo Friday as investors waited for the government to announce 
details of its economic stimulus package. The Nikkei index of 225 
selected stocks moved in a narrow range for most of the trading session, 
and then dropped sharply in the final minutes of trading. The Nikkei 
fell 219 points. In Hong Kong, the Hang Seng index dropped 82 points, 
the Australian All Ordinaries gained at 25. On the currency exchange 
market in Tokyo, the dollar gained slightly against the yen, but it 
was lower against the mark. The dollar was quoted at 118.27 yen in 
the Far East, and at 1.6595 marks, slightly lower than the late quote 
Thursday in New York.

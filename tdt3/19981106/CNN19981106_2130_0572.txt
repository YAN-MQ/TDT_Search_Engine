Fitting child safety seats into some cars can be tricky, but that 
soon could change. "The Washington Post" reports the White House will 
unveil a universal system for installing the seats next month. The 
paper says the plan would take effect as early as next year for some 
models. According to federal statistics 80% of children strapped into 
safety seats are at risk because those seats are improperly installed.

Tonight, the tragic death of one of America's most gifted film directors 
alan pakula. Killed today when a loose pipe on a long island highway 
was accidentally catapulted into his windshield. Pakula, a stylish 
Ivy leaguer, directed contemporary American classics such as "klute" 
and "all the president's men," for which he won an academy award. 
His widow, Hannah, is a well-known author of historical biographies. 
And together they were one of the most stylish couples on the New 
York scene.

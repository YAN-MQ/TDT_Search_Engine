Elsewhere in the world, Serbia has reacted negatively to a U.S. peace 
proposal for its troubled Kosovo province. The president of Yugoslavia's 
dominant republic says the plan would violate Serbia's territorial 
integrity and unduly favor Kosovo's Ethnic Albanian majority. A leading 
Ethnica Albanian newspaper published excerpts of the draft proposal 
authored by U.S. envoy Christopher Hill. The newspaper says it outlines 
virtual self rule for Kosovo.

Manchester United said Tuesday it would increase the capacity of Old 
Trafford to 67,400, making it the biggest club stadium in Britain.

at a cost of 30 million pounds (dlrs 50 million) with work expected 
to be finished by the start of the 2000-2001 season. With the increase, 
Old Trafford will surpass Celtic Park in Glasgow, which seats 60,300.

News agency reports from Congo Kinshasa say government forces killed 
up to 600 rebels in a weekend air strike on boats on Lake Tanganyika. 
Reuters quotes State Radio as saying the rebels died when their boats 
were targeted by planes from Zimbabwe which is allied with the Congolese 
government. The French news agency quotes unnamed independent sources 
as saying the boats were transporting rebel military equipment between 
Kalemie and Moba.

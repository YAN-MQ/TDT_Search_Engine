Yugoslavia's men's volleyball team has upset Olympic champion The 
Netherlands in the quarter final round of the world championships 
in Japan. The Yugoslavs scored 15 points in a row to take the second 
set and took the match 3 to nothing. The win leaves Yugoslavia undefeated 
after five games. However, Italy posted a 3-to-1 win over Russia and 
remains in first place over Yugoslavia in Group H. In other matches 
in Group H, Ukraine beat the United States 3 to 2, Greece scored its 
first win of the tournament, a 3-to-1 victory over China. Now in Group 
G, Brazil remains undefeated, thanks to Tuesdays 3-to-nothing win 
over Cuba. Spain is in second place in the group after a 3-1 win over 
Argentina. Only the top two teams from each group will be advancing 
into the final medal round.

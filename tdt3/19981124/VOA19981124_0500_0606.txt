Also, from the Balkans. Croatian president Franjo Tudjman is on a 
rare foreign visit to Greece. Mr. Tudjman began his three-day official 
trip Monday, meeting in Athens with Greek President Kostos Stepanopolis 
and Prime Minister Kostos Semitas. He thanked the Greek leaders for 
their contribution in bringing an end to the fighting in the Balkans. 
President Tudjman and Prime Minister Semitas, also signed cooperation 
agreements on tourism, drug trafficking, and crime fighting.

Striking union workers at anheuser-Busch will be back on the job Monday 
after a one-day walkout. 65 employees struck Thursday at the St. Louis,

After several hours of talks, the union decided to continue contract 
negotiations with management. One union leader says a misunderstanding 
caused the strike.

Clocks in Lebanon will be moved back one hour at midnight Saturday, 
ending seven months of daylight saving summer time. This puts Lebanon 
two hours ahead of Greenwich Mean Time. Standard time remains in effect 
until the last weekend of March 1999, according to a Cabinet decision.

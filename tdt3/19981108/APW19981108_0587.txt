A family was killed on a highway southern Greece Sunday when a truck 
driven by a Portuguese slammed head-on into a private car in slippery 
road conditions, police said. The accident occurred near the town 
of Aegio, 178 kilometers (110 miles) west of Athens. Police said a 
Greek couple, their two-year-old son and a 15-year-old girl all died 
and their bodies were charred by fire. The driver, identified as 35-year-old 
Alfonso Pavlo Dacosia Ferriera Sao Pedro, was being held by police 
in Aegio.

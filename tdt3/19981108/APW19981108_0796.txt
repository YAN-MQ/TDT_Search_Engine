Freshman center Kris Lang scored 16 points and Terrence Newby had 
10 assists, leading No. 11 North Carolina to a 90-71 victory over 
the Australian All-Stars Sunday. Brendan Haywood scored 14, Ademola 
Okulaja 13, Vasco Evtimov 12 and Brian Bersticker 10 for the Tar Heels. 
North Carolina hit 37-of-59 shots (63 percent) and outrebounded the 
Australians 41-25. The Australians led 38-35 at halftime. But the 
Tar Heels made 24-of-32 shots in the second half to take command. 
Haywood and Evtimov each grabbed seven rebounds.

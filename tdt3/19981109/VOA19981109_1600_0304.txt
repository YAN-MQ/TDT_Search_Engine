The United States is urging India and Pakistan to do more to ease 
the danger of nuclear arms proliferation in South Asia. A White House 
spokesman says President Clinton wants the two rivals to sign the 
Comprehensive Nuclear Test Ban Treaty to stop producing materials 
that could be used for nuclear weapons and to restrain development 
of missiles and aircraft capable of carrying nuclear war heads. Last 
week, Washington lifted some on the two countries.

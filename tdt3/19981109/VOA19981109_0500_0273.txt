Cambodian King Nordam Seinoc has celebrated the 45-Anniversay of independence 
from France with a wreath laying ceremony. The 76-year old king appeared 
in good spirits despite suffering from a number of ailments. He is 
to travel to Beijing at the end of the week for two months of medical 
treatment. Opposition politicians have not yet responded the king's 
offer made Sunday to preside over talks on a new government using 
his palace and security detail for their protection.

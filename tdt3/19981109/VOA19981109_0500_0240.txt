The trial of Malayasia's Former Deputy Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim 
has begun its second week. Prosecutors rejected defense claims that 
a police report named several top officers involved in a plot against

exist and residing judge told prosecutors to produce an active police 
official to testify on the matter. Mr. Anwar has pleaded not quilt 
to 10 charges to illegal sex and corruption and says he is the victim 
of a political conspiracy.

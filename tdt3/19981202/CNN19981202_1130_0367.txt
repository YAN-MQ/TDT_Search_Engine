Microsoft chairman Bill Gates is reportedly donating $100 million 
to help immunize children in developing countries. "The Washington 
Post" reports, the money will be given through the William h. Gates

already protect american children. Diseases such as hepatitis B and 
rotavirus kill millions of children in developing countries each year.

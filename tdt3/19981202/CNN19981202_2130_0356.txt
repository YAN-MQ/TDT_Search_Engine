A jury in Washington has acquitted former agriculture secretary Mike 
Espy of all 30 corruption charges. The jurors deliberated for nine

tickets and other gifts from companies he regulated. Independent counsel 
Donald smaltz spent nearly $20 million and four years investigating 
the allegations.

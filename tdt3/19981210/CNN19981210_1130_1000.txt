More consolidation in the struggling managed care industry. I'm Myron 
Kandel in New York with this edition of "Dollars & Sense." Aetna has 
agreed to buy the health care business of Prudential insurance for 
$1 billion in cash. This acquisition is Aetna's latest move in its 
transformation from an insurance to managed care company. After adding 
the 6.5 million members, it will be the largest provider of health 
care benefits in the country, with more than 22 million members.

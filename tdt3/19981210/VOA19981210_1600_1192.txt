Common Wealth Games pole vault silver medalist Dennis Pechowski, of 
New Zealand has been stripped of his medal. And banned from the 2002 
Common Wealth Games. The Russian-born vaulter received the punishment 
Thursday. After the Game's federation found him guilty of taking performance-enhancing 
steroids. The action means that Australia's Paul Burgess is elevated 
from the bronze to silver medal. And fourth place finisher Kersely 
Gardine of Marrakech is upgraded to the bronze medal.

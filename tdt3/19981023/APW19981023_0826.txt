Peru and Ecuador have agreed on terms to end a six-decade border dispute, 
diplomatic sources from both countries said Friday. Presidents Alberto 
Fujimori of Peru and Jamil Mahuad of Ecuador will sign a peace accord 
on Monday here in Brazil's capital, where the negotiations took place, 
said the sources from both countries, who asked not to be identified 
pending an official announcement.

Baseball fans are looking forward to game one of the World Series 
on Saturday between the New York Yankees and the San Diego Padres. 
On Thursday, thousands of enthusiastic Yankee fans held a rally in 
New York City Hall Park. VOA's Martin Bush reports. When not feasting 
on free hot dogs, strawberry cake, peanuts and soda, New York Yankee 
fans were cheering their team at a noontime rally. Especially when 
ace picture, David Cohen told them exactly what they wanted to hear. 
To all the great Yankee fans out there, New York is the baseball capital 
of the world. You all deserve the World Series. We brought it to you 
now lets go get it. Thank you. New York's Mayor, Rudolph Giuliani, 
who considers himself the cities number one Yankee fan got a little 
ahead of himself in his remarks. We're all going to be back here for 
a great celebration when the Yankees win the World Series. The first 
two games of the best of seven World Series were at Yankees Stadium 
the next three in San Diego. Martin Bush, VOA News, New York.

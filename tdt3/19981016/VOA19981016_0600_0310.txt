Meanwhile, India and Pakistan has pledged to pursue a positive approach 
to their high level talks in Islamabad, which is set to begun later 
today. Indian Foreign Secretary said he's committed to improving relations 
in three days of talks with his Pakistani counterpart, Shamshed Ahmad. 
Mr. Ahmad said Pakistan wants to dialogue with India that will allow 
both countries to live in peace with each other. The two sides are 
expected to discuss nuclear issues as well as a disputed region of 
Kashmir during their meetings.

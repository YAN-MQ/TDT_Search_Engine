The Russian parliament called Friday for prompt aid to the Khabarovsk 
region in the Far East that has been damaged by forest fires. The 
fires has burned more than 1.5 million hectares (3.7 million acres) 
of forest and done irreparable damage to plants and animals in the 
region, the ITAR-Tass news agency said. The region has spent more 
than 40 million rubles (dlrs 2.5 million) to fight the fires, it said. 
The fires are expected to burn until rain or snow extinguishes them.

Taiwan's top negotiator with China has arrived in Shanghai for the 
first high level talks between the two sides in five years. Koo Chun 
Fu, head of Taipei's official group dealing with Beijing will meet 
with his mainland counterpart Wong Dung Hung. Mr. Koo will meet later 
this week with Chinese President Jiang Zemin. No breakthroughs are 
expected but Mr. Koo's trip is seen as an important step in improving 
relations.

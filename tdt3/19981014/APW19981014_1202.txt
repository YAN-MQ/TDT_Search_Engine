The French company Alstom has signed a contract to supply Turkey with 
an electricity plant worth 2.1 billion francs (dlrs 380 million), 
the company said Wednesday. The coal-fired plant will be located in 
the Canakkale region of western Turkey. Alstom will supply the turbines, 
alternators as well as the control system and electricity distribution 
system, Alstom announced.

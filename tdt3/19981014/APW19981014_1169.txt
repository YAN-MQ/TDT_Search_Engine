Floods unleashed by heavy rains have killed 63 people in northwestern 
Sudan, the government said Wednesday. The Interior Ministry said the 
floods occurred Monday in the province of Sodiri, 400 Kilometers (250 
miles) west of Khartoum. A large number of livestock also died, it 
said. Local officials are provide assistance, it said. No other details 
were given.

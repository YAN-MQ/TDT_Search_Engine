Could a record-setting sports sale be in the works? NBC reports cablevision 
systems is about to buy the New York Yankees. Owner George Steinbrenner 
is reportedly willing to part with the Bronx bombers for $600 million. 
He bought the world champion Yankees for $10 million 25 years ago. 
The deal would take effect on January 1st, with Steinbrenner continuing 
to run the team. It would be the most expensive sale ever of a U.S. 
sports franchise.

What's white and fluffy and big enough to fill three 18-wheelers? 
Would you believe 7,838-cubic feet of popcorn? Students from elementary 
schools in fayette county, Georgia, along with a Theater company from 
Dallas, popped the corn to get into "the Guinness book of world records" 
for the largest container of popcorn. The giant tub was 34 feet in 
diameter and four feet high, and the students did set a world record.

A memorial is being erected to the victims of valujet flight 592. 
Ground was broken yesterday for the memorial, which will be located 
near the crash site in everglades national park in Florida. It will 
be made up of rows of coral stone columns, which will create a Sloping 
triangle. It's scheduled to be dedicated May 11th, the third anniversary 
of the crash that killed 110 people.

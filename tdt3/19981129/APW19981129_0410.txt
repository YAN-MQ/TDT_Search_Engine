Zimbabwe's Nick Price won the Zimbabwe Open on Sunday, shooting a 
final round of a nine-under-par 63 on the Royal Harare golf course 
for a 72-hole total of 17-under-par, or 271. South Africa's Tjaart 
van er Walt was second, five shots back on 276 after a closing 68. 
South African Hennie Walters, who led by one shot on Saturday, took 
third with 278 after a 71.

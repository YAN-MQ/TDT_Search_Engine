Pope John Paul II presided over a three-hour ceremony at St. Peter's 
basilica today to Mark the official start of church preparations for 
the millenium. The Pope declared the year 2000 a holy year and read

He tells Roman Catholics how they might get to heaven faster. Among 
them -- visit the sick or jailed, give to the poor and make pilgrimages. 
Also included, abstaining from cigarettes and alcohol.

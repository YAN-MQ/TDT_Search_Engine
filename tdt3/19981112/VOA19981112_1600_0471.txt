Russia's defense minister, Igor Sergeyev will go to the Mid East Friday. 
He'll meet with Syrian and Egyptian officials for talks on the Iraqi

serious consequences on long-term weapons monitoring in Iraq. The 
foreign minister called on Iraq to comply with U.N. weapons inspectors.

Hurricane Madeline continues to steam toward Mexico's western pacific 
coast, while hurricane Lester is weakening and moving out to sea.

shipments of aid supplies to the area. Meanwhile, hurricane Lester's 
winds fell to about 90 miles per hour, and a Previous hurricane warning

BULLETIN-IMMEDIATE BROADCAST REQUESTED URBAN AND SMALL STREAM FLOOD 
ADVISORY NATIONAL WEATHER SERVICE SAN JUAN PR 432 PM AST MON OCT 19 
1998 ...THE NATIONAL WEATHER SERVICE IN SAN JUAN PR HAS ISSUED AN 
URBAN AND SMALL STREAM FLOOD ADVISORY UNTIL 600 PM AST FOR THE WESTERN 
MUNICIPALITY MAYAGUEZ... AT 425 PM...DOPPLER RADAR SHOWED AN AREA 
OF THUNDERSTORMS WITH VERY HEAVY RAIN ACROSS WESTERN PUERTO RICO OVER 
MAYAGUEZ...LAS MARIAS AND MARICAO. THE THUNDERSTORMS HAVE CONTINUED 
TO REDEVELOP OVER THE SAME AREA THROUGHOUT THE AFTERNOON. DOPPLER 
WEATHER RADAR ESTIMATES 3 TO 4 INCHES OF RAINFALL HAS OCCURED OVER 
MAYAGUEZ DURING THE PAST THREE HOURS. GROUNDS ACROSS THIS SECTION 
OF PUERTO RICO REMAINED VERY WET FROM RECENT HEAVY RAINS. ALTHOUGH 
THERE WERE NO REPORTS OF WIDESPREAD FLOODING...LOCALIZED STREAM..STREET 
AND ROAD FLOODING WILL BE LIKELY ACROSS THIS AREA. THEREFORE AN URBAN 
AND SMALL STREAM ADVISORY HAS BEEN ISSUED UNTIL 6 PM FOR MAYAGUEZ. 
MOTORISTS AND PEDESTRIANS IN OR PASSING THROUGH THIS AREA SHOULD AVOID 
CROSSING FLOODED ROADS...AND ALLOW EXTRA TIME TO REACH THEIR DESTINATIONS. 
VISIBILITIES ARE GREATLY REDUCED IN HEAVY RAIN. REMEMBER...CARS CAN 
EASILY STALL IN THE WATER AND BE CARRIED AWAY IN ANY CURRENT. PEDESTRIANS 
ARE ALSO ADVISED TO STAY AWAY FROM RISING CREEKS. AVOID STEEP TERRAIN 
WHERE MUDSLIDES ARE POSSIBLE.

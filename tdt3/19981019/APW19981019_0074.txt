No.1-ranked Pete Sampras will headline the eight-player field at the 
Colonial Classic in the week before the 1999 Australian Open. Sampras 
will be joined by Australian US Open runner-up Mark Philippoussis, 
Andre Agassi, Yevgeny Kafelnikov, Goran Ivanisevic and Thomas Enqvist. 
Two other players will be added to the field. The Colonial Classic 
will be played Jan. 13-16 with the Australian Open starting Jan. 18.

Do your actions live up to your words? A new survey suggests for teenagers 
the answer often is no. The character counts coalition surveyed 20,000 
middle and high school students. 8% of them agreed that it's important 
to have good character. However, asked whether they had cheated on 
a test 70% said they had at least once and 44% said they had more 
than once 47% said they have stolen something from a store at least 
once 27% say they've shoplifted more than once.

Nadezhda Petrova rallied from one-set down to upset Iva Majoli 6-7 
(5-7), 7-6 (7-5), 6-0 Tuesday in the first round of the Ladies Kremlin 
Cup. Petrova, ranked 175th in the world, trailed the 20th-ranked Majoli 
5-2 in the second set before rallying to win 11 of the last 12 games. 
Petrova, a 16-year-old Russian playing just her second professional 
event, qualified as a wild-card for the tournament on Monday. In other 
results Tuesday, Barbara Schett of Austria beat German Anke Huber 
6-2, 6-3. Spain's Magui Serni beat American Lisa Raymond 5-7, 6-3, 
6-4. Natasha Zvereva of Belarus beat Russian Tatiana Panova and Russian 
Elena Likhovtseva knocked out Ruxandra Dragomir of Romania.

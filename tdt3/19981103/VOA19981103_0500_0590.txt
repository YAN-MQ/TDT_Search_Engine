Japan has agreed to give more than $38 million in food assistance 
to Indonesia. Tokyo will extend $26 million to help pay for food imports 
for Indonesia's poor. The other $12 million will go towards increasing 
food production. The money will purchase items such as pesticides, 
fertilizers, tractors, and irrigation pumps.

Subfreezing temperatures and icy conditions forced officials Friday 
to call off three Bundesliga games scheduled for Saturday _ Hertha 
Berlin vs. Freiburg, MSV Duisburg vs. FC Kaiserslautern and Borussia 
Moenchengladbach vs. Werder Bremen. Hertha Berlin vs. Freiburg and 
Moenchengladbach vs. Bremen will be replayed Tuesday, Dec. 8, while 
Duisburg will host Kaiserslautern Dec. 16.

Cadillac may be about to lose its crown in the luxury car sales race. 
It has been America's most popular luxury car line for 48 years, but 
it's about to be taken over by Lincoln. Analysts say Cadillac sales 
have been hurt by its late entry of a sport utility vehicle, and by 
disappointing efforts to shed its stuffy image. A company official 
acknowledges Cadillac may even wind up in third place next year, behind 
Mercedes-Benz.

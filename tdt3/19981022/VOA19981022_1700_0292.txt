A powerful Typhoon has clobbered the Philippines. More from Ruth Youngblood. 
One week after Typhoon Zeb left 83 people dead in the Northern Philippines, 
Typhoon Babs has belted the country with gusts up to 200 kilometers 
per hour and torrential rains. Typhoon-weary residents of low lying 
and coastal areas were advised even before the onslaught to seek shelter 
on higher ground. That was Correspondent Ruth Youngblood.

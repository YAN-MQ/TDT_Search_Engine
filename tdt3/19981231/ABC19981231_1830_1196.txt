One more note about "your money." The new year brings a couple of 
new tax breaks, grab a sharp pencil. Parents in the middle income 
tax bracket will now be able to deduct $500 for each child under 17, 
up from $400. For parents with kids in college, the IRS will offer 
tuition credits up to $1,500 for the first two years of college. And 
the 2 million people who use their home as an office will be able 
to claim a deduction for the first time.

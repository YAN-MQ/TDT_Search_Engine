The Russian government has earmarked dlrs 8.6 million in humanitarian 
aid to Armenia, the ITAR-Tass news agency reported Thursday. Prime 
Minister Yevgeny Primakov ordered that the money be allocated to commemorate 
the earthquake that devastated Armenia 10 years ago, killing 25,000 
people and ruining or heavily damaging 21 towns and 324 villages, 
ITAR-Tass said, citing the government press office.

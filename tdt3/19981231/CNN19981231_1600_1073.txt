Microsoft, currently being sued by the justice department and several 
states for possible antitrust abuses, is now the plaintiff in a new 
legal battle. The software company is suing two men from Texas who 
have registered internet domain names, including Microsoft windows.com 
and Microsoft office.com. the suit filed in federal court in Houston 
claims the men are infringing on Microsoft trademarks and misleading 
the public. Microsoft seeks unspecified damages, all profits from 
the sites and full possession of the domain names.

Sara Lee corporation is recalling hot dogs and other packaged meats 
that may contain dangerous bacteria. They include products sold nationally 
under well-known brands like ball park, Bryan, hygrade, bil Mar, grillmaster, 
and Mr. Turkey the affected products are coded est p261 and est 6911. 
Executives at Sara Lee say they are acting on reports the meat products 
could contain listeria bacteria. Healthy people can usually fight 
off the bacteria, but it can be deadly for the elderly and people 
with weak immune systems.

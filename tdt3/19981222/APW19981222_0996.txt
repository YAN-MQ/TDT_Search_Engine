Ferrari, the Italian car racer and luxury sports car maker, said Tuesday 
that the U.S. toymaker Mattel, Inc. has been granted exclusive rights 
to produce toys and adult collectibles based on Ferrari's cars and 
race team. The first products are to be unveiled in February at the 
toy fair in Nuremberg, Germany, Ferrari said. Mattel is based in California.

And, finally, Ford is recalling more than three million of its vehicles 
sold in the United States and Canada. They want to check for possible 
corrosion. The recall list includes some top-selling models, like 
the Taurus and the Mercury Sable. A Ford spokesman says that, in less 
than 1 percent of the vehicles involved, supports for the engine and 
transmission might have been corroded by road salt and moisture.

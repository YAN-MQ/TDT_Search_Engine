Russian Prime Minister Yevgeny Primakov said Saturday that if NATO 
uses force to try to resolve the Kosovo crisis, Russia may have to

for international cooperation in an interview with the NTV television

was approved on March 31, which imposed an arms embargo on Yugoslavia.

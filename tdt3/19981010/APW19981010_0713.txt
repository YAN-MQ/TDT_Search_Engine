Israel routed San Marino's collection of amateur players 5-0 Saturday 
night in a Group 6 Euro 2000 qualifier. Israel led 3-0 at halftime, 
on goals by Haim Ravivo 16 minutes after kickoff, Avi Nimni two minutes 
later and Alon Mizrachi in the 31st. San Marino defender Mauro Valentini 
scored an own-goal to make it 4-0 in the 58th, and Israel's Najuan 
Geryev tallied with eight minutes left to fix the final score before 
about 1,000 spectators. The lineups: San Marino _ Federico Gasperoni; 
Mirco Gennari, Mauro Marani, Mauro Valentini, Simone Bacciocchi (substitute: 
Vittorio Valentini, 63rd), William Guerra; Riccardo Muccioli, Pier 
Domenico Della Valle (Fabio Francini, 67th), Paolo Montagna (Davide 
Gualtieri, 80th), Ivan Matteoni; Andy Selva. Israel _ Rafi Cohen; 
Ronen Harazi, Najuan Geryev, Arik Benado (Amir Shelah, 70th), Ran 
Ben-Shimon; Jean Talasnikov, Walid Badir, Haim Ravivo, Eyal Berkovic; 
Avi Nimni, Alon Mizrachi.

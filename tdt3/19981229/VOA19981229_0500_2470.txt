Officials in Yemen say armed tribesmen have kidnapped 16 western tourists 
Monday in the southern province of Abiyon. The victims included 12 
Britons, two Americans, and two Australians. Details about the abduction 
are unclear. Three weeks ago, guardsmen abducted four German tourists. 
They're still being held. Yemeni tribesman often kidnap foreigners 
to pressure the government and oil companies working in the country 
to improve conditions in tribal areas.

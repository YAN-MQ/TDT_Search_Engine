Last year, cashmere was considered a luxury. This season you can find 
something in cashmere at just about any price you want to spend. Lord 
&AMP; Taylor has cashmere gloves ($30), berets ($40), mufflers ($90), 
pullovers ($75 to $80) and cardigans ($100 to $120), all in a range 
of colors. The Banana Republic stores have hats ($35), scarves ($65), 
V-neck shells ($88), T-shirts ($98), vests ($158), cardigans ($178) 
and long-sleeve crew necks ($168), in colors like pale gray, charcoal, 
plum, black and currant. N. Peal, 5 West 56th St., has pompom booty 
socks ($65) and anklets ($75) in pastels and pale gray. At Barneys 
New York, a V-neck pullover in beige, gray, black or heather blue 
is $198; a hat in red, teal or yellow is $115. Also at Barneys, a 
spaghetti-strap camisole by Aquah in pink, gray, red or black is $145, 
and a three-quarter-sleeve pullover with a deep V-neck by IS in pale 
blue or navy is $235.

Norwegian skiers Tor Arne Hetland and Bente Martinsen again dominated 
a cross-country world cup sprint Monday after both won in the last 
event. In the final of the nighttime men's event, raced over 1900 
meters, Hetland finished ahead of Austria's Christian Hoffmann and 
Patrick Maechler of Switzerland. In the women's final, Martinsen beat 
fellow Norwegian Maj Helen Sorkmo. Italy's Karin Moroder came in third. 
Hetland and Martinsen lead the men's and women's World Cup sprint 
standings. Both won in a 1400-meter sprint event Sunday in Garmisch-Partenkirchen, 
Germany.

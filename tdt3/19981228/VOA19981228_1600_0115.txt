Officials in Yemen say, armed tribesman have kidnapped 16 western 
tourists in the south of the country. The victims: 12 Britons, two 
Americans and two Australians were kidnapped Monday in the southern 
province of Abion. Details about the abduction remain unclear but 
diplomats and Yemeni officials say the tourists may have been taken 
to an unknown destination in Abion. Tribesmen in Yemen have often 
kidnapped foreigners to pressure the government and oil companies 
working in the country to improve infrastructure in tribal areas.

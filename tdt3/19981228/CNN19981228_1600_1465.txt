Japan held a special ceremony today, to say "good-bye" to the depressing 
year of the Tiger and "hello" to good luck and the year of the rabbit. 
A year-old rabbit and a baby Bengal Tiger were brought together in 
Osaka to mark the occasion. In 1998, the year of the Tiger, Japan 
had its worst recession since world war II. 1999 is the year of the 
rabbit and according to the Chinese calendar that should bring prosperity.

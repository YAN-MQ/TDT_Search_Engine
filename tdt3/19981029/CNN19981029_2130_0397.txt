The food and drug administration has approved the use of Tamoxifen 
as a Possible way to prevent breast cancer in women who are at very 
high risk of developing the disease. Tamoxifen carries powerful side 
effects including a higher risk of uterine cancer and potentially 
fatal blood clots. Women are cautioned to undergo careful screening 
before taking the drug. Tamoxifen is the first ever drug approved 
as a cancer preventative.

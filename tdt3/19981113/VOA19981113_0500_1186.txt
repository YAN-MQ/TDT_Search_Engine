Reigning Olympic Champion Cuba has defeated China in straight sets 
to take the Women's World Volleyball Championship in Japan. Cuba needed 
only 15minutes to take the first set, 15-4. But the Chinese fought 
back in the second set, tying the Cubans 10 times through 14. However, 
the Cubans took the last two points and the set, 16-14. The Chinese 
fought hard in the third set, but could not overcome the Cuban attack, 
and lost 15 -12. In the third place game, Russia beat Brazil 3-1. 
Italy took fifth place by defeating Croatia 3-0. The Netherlands took 
the seventh place game by beating Japan 3-1.

German Chancellor Gerhard Schroeder began talks with Russian Prime 
Minister Yevgeny Primakov on Monday. Central Bank Chairman Viktor 
Gerashchenko and other top-ranking officials also attended the meeting. 
The visit is Schroeder's first to Russia as Germany's chancellor. 
Russian officials are expected to press Schroeder for more Western 
aid to deal with the country's crippling economic crisis, the Russian 
Foreign Ministry said.

Sigurd Rushfeldt scored all goals as Rosenborg beat Galatasaray 3-0 
in the third round of the European Champions League Wednesday Rushfeldt 
scored the first goal of the game in the 68th minute after outmaneuvering 
a couple of Galatasaray defenders in the box. A minute later Rushfeldt 
was alone with goalie Claudio Taffarel, but the shot went inches outside 
the goal. ``Totto'' Dahlum won a fight in the box in the 85th, sent 
the ball to Borge Hernes who made a shot. Rushfeldt took advantage 
of the rebound to make it 2-0. Rushfeldt finally scored the third 
goal easily in the 90th when he was alone with the goalie. Bjorn Otto 
Bragstad was booked in the 50th and will miss the next game. Inceefe 
Vedat got an early shower in the 80th when he got his second yellow 
card of the game.

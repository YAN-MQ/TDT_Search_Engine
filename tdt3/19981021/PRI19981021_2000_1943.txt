Italy has a new government. Former Communist Masamo Delama has sworn 
in as prime minister. Eight ministers, among them, the treasury and 
foreign ministers, are the same as in the previous government of Romano 
Pradi. Member of parliament Carlo Leone, outlines the new government's 
priorities. First, approving the budget law. The second is fighting 
the unemployment, especially in the south of Italy. The third one 
will be proceeding in the work over changing our constitution to make 
the Italian political system more stable. Carolo Leone.

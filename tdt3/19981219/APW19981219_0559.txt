Iraq's vice president declared Saturday that Baghdad will end all 
dealings with the U.N. Special Commission, which oversees inspections 
of Iraqi weapons as required by the United Nations, in response to 
the American and British airstrikes. Taha Yassin Ramadan told a news 
conference that ``the Special Commission is now behind us. Its mission 
is over.''

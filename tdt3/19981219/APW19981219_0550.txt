Iraq's vice president declared Saturday that the Baghdad regime would 
end all dealings with the U.N. Special Commission, which oversees 
weapons inspections, in response to the American and British air strikes. 
Taha Yassin Ramadan told a news conference that ``the Special Commission 
is now behind us. Its mission is over.''

All may not be well in the magic kingdom. "The orange county Register" 
reports complaints are on the rise at disneyland. Customers are apparently 
upset over price increases, reduced hours and parking difficulties. 
Disney had cut the operating hours of some attractions, but reversed 
the decision after a flood of complaints. Disneyland's president says 
the criticism is part of the job, but says the park is still a good 
value.

Marcelo Bielsa will debut as Argentina's new coach on Feb. 1 against 
Venezuela in Miami, the Argentine Football Association announced Wednesday. 
The match is one of two confirmed games for Argentina in the United 
States in Miami. Three days after playing Venezuela, Argentina is 
to face Guatemala, also in Miami. The United States plays Germany 
in Jacksonville, Florida, on Feb. 6 and Germany will then face Colombia 
on Feb. 9 in Miami, the U.S. federation announced Wednesday. Bielsa 
takes over for Daniel Passarella, who resigned as previously announced 
following the World Cup.

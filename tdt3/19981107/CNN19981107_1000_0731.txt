Many health experts say it's the first installment of one of the best 
online medical resources available. "USA today" reports a comprehensive 
emergency medical textbook is being linked to Internet search engines

symptoms. The site then suggests several possible causes of the problem. 
400 doctors contributed to the site. Drug companies are sponsoring 
links.

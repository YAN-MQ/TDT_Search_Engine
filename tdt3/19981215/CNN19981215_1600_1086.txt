Cereal lovers will have to dish out more money for cornflakes, rice 
krispies and other Kellogg products. The nation's largest cereal maker 
raised prices an average of 2.7%. the price of a box of cornflakes, 
its best-selling cereal, rose 6%. Bite-size frosted miniwheats jumped 
11%. But low-fat granola prices dropped 11%. Kellogg says this is 
its first price increase since 1994.

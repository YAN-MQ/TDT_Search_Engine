Chinese athletes have taken gold medals in the men's and women's table 
tennis competition at the Asian games in Bangkok. In the women's singles 
final, Wang Nan won the gold by defeating compatriot Lee Zhu in three 
games. In the men's doubles final, China's Kong Lin Hui and Lee Huang 
beat South Korea's Lee Chow Swang and Osang Yen 21-17, 21-13.

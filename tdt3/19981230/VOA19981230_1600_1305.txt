German ski jumper, Martin Schmidt has edged Austria's Andreas Goldberger 
to win the first of the Four Hills competitions in Oberstoff, Germany. 
The 20-year-old Schmidt gathered 244.8 points. That's just two ahead 
of Goldberger. It was Schmidt's fifth win this season. The Four Hills 
Tournament is regarded ski jumping's most coveted prize outside the 
Olympics or World Championships.

An Air France plane on a regular domestic flight between Lyon and 
Nantes lost a wheel after takeoff Wednesday but made an emergency 
landing safely, the Lyon airport said. The Boeing 737-300 plane, with 
59 people on board, landed at Lyon-Satolas airport about 10:20 a.m. 
(0920 GMT), the airport said. The flight, AF7863, was to have flown 
from Lyon to the western French city of Nantes. The rear left wheel 
of the plane fell off just after takeoff.

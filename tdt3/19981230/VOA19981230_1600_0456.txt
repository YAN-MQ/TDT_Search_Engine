Iraqi newspapers have lashed out at moderate Arab governments for 
the postponement of an Arab League foreign ministers meeting that 
was to discuss the issue of economic sanctions. The newspapers say 
the moderates are seeking to foil the wishes of the Arab masses as 
expressed in angry demonstrations in nearly a dozen Arab nations during 
the strikes. Scott Bob, VOA News, Middle East bureau.

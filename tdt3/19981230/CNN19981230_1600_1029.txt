Daimler-Chrysler is recalling more than 700,000 vehicles because of 
a Potential fire hazard in the fuel injection system. The recall was 
posted on the web site of the national highway traffic safety administration. 
The recall covers the Chrysler Concord, LHS and New Yorker models 
as well as the Dodge intrepid and eagle vision. It covers cars made 
from June 1992 to July of 1997. The posting did not say if any injuries 
have occurred.

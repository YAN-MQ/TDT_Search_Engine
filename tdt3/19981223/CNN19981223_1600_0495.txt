Four people were killed this morning in a high-rise fire in New York 
City. Firefighters say it started on the 19th floor of the 51-story 
building near lincoln center. According to recent court papers, the

floor. The victims were found in the halls of the 27th and 29th floors.

the city's second fatal high-rise fire in a week.

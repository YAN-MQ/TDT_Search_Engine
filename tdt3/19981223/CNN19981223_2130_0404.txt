President Clinton says he wants to end homelessness in the United 
States. Speaking from a public-housing project in Baltimore, Mr. Clinton 
said he will request an additional $150 million in his next budget 
proposal. In order to build affordable housing for the homeless, he 
also announced $850 million in federal grants, earmarked for homeless 
assistance programs. Mr. Clinton says the measure is the largest effort 
to combat homelessness in U.S. history.

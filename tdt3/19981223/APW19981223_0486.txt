Three British tourists, two women and a man, were killed when their 
car crashed into a tree early Wednesday, police said. Police identified 
the three as Stella Mildred Walter, 51, Judith Diana Jones, 50 and 
Frank Jones, 53. A police statement said the accident in this southern 
coastal resort town occurred at about 3:30 a.m. (0130 gmt). The driver 
of the car, Carl Edward Troy, 31, was seriously injured, the statement 
said. It gave no other details.

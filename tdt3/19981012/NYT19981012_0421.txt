Leonard Forman has been named president and chief executive of The 
New York Times Co.'s magazine group, the company announced on Monday. 
Formerly senior vice president for corporate development, Forman, 
53, succeeds James Fitzgerald, 59, who will serve in an advisory role 
as chairman of the magazine group for one year. Forman will be responsible 
for the magazine group's publications: Golf Digest, Golf World and 
Golf Shop Operations. He will report to Russell Lewis, president and 
chief executive of the Times Co. The Times Co. publishes The New York 
Times, The Boston Globe, 21 regional newspapers and three magazines. 
It also operates television and radio stations as well as news, photo, 
graphic and electronic publishing services.

Microsoft under investigation and facing a court trial on government 
charges of violating antitrust laws in the U.S. is also having difficulties 
in Japan. The Japanese Fair Trade Commission has issued a warning 
to the software maker's Japanese unit. Japan's watchdog unit raided 
Microsoft offices just after the justice department began its investigation. 
The Japanese investigation centers on Microsoft's inclusion of its 
Internet Explorer in its Windows '95 operating system, much the same 
as the investigation in the U.S.

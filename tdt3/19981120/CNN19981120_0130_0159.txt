Pollsters are already weighing in on Ken Starr's testimony. 652 people 
were questioned between 6:00 P.M. to 9:00 P.M. on Thursday night for 
the latest CNN/"USA today" Gallup poll. 26% of respondents said Starr's 
presentation was excellent. 41% rated it good. % said it was "not 
so good." And 12% rated the presentation poor. The poll also asked 
people if the impeachment hearings accomplish something worthwhile. 
43% of the respondents said "yes." 49% said "no."

Kids between the ages of 4 and 15 are most likely to die in an auto 
accident because many of them don't buckle up. Those government statistics

shoulder harnesses stretch across a child's neck and lap restraints 
could damage internal organs in an accident. A federal panel is being 
asked for recommendations.

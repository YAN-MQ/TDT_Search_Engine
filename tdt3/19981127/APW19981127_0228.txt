Nineteen people in Siberia have been hospitalized with food poisoning 
after consuming a potent liquid solution used as a vitamin supplement 
on chicken farms, a news report said Friday. The solution was stolen 
from a farm and sold as vegetable oil at a market in Ulan Ude, in 
southern Siberia, near the Mongolian border, the ITAR-Tass news agency 
reported. Among those who consumed the product were the suspected 
thief and his family, and they were also among those hospitalized, 
the report said. Several victims were in serious condition, the report 
added.

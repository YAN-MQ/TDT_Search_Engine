Bosnian Serb experts have begun the first exhumation of a suspected 
mass grave, containing the bodies of Serb civilians in a cemetery 
in Sarajevo. The head of the commission on missing persons for the 
Serb Republic in Bosnia Herzegovina. It says that about ten bodies 
were discovered at the site in the first few hours. The official says 
his group believes the grave in Sarajevo's Lyon Cemetery may contain 
as many as 150 bodies. Excavation teams first removed bodies from 
individual graves of Sarajevans killed during the Bosnian conflict 
to get to the site where witnesses pointed out the location of the 
mass grave.

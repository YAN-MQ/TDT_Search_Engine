The golden arches are making their way to the big screen. McDonald's 
is expanding its marketing blitz tied to the Walt Disney film, "A 
bug's life." It's going beyond its usual happy meal promotions and

the fast-food chain in a number of recent TV shows on its ABC network. 
Analysts expect the expanded relationship will be worth millions for 
both companies.

Brian Donaldson, a career diplomat, will take over in January as high 
commissioner to Namibia, the Foreign Office said Monday. Donaldson, 
52, is now in the Foreign Office's information department. His career 
includes diplomatic postings in Bangladesh, Cameroun, Mauritius, Luxembourg, 
Nigeria and Algeria. He will replace Glyn Davies, whose next appointment 
was not announced.

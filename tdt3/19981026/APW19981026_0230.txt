Algis Zhuraitis, music conductor of the Bolshoi Theater, has died, 
Russian news agencies said Monday. He was 70. Zhuraitis had suffered 
from a ``prolonged grave illness,'' ITAR-Tass said, quoting the theater's 
press service. Interfax said he died Sunday. They gave no other details.

In women's tennis, third seed, Elenis Borlea of Romania and seventh 
seed, Eva Mioli of Croas have easily reached the second round of the 
Siot Open in Luxembourg. Borlea ousted Marion Ormans of the Netherlands, 
6-3,6-1. While Mioli beat Lucinda Drydamier of Romania 6-2, 6-4. The 
only other seeded player in action Monday was number eight, Henrietta 
Nyoma of Slovakia, who was upset by Italian, Sylvia Fauna in two sets. 
Top seed, Natalie Tosia and fellow French and second seed, Mary Pierce 
have first-round buys. Defending champion, Amanda Cuther of South 
Africa is the fourth seed.

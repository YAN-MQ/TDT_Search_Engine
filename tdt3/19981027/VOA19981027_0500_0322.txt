The National Hurricane Center in Miami has upgraded Hurricane Mitch 
to a Category Five storm capable of catastrophic damage. On Monday, 
Mitch grew into one of the strongest Atlantic storms ever recorded 
threatening Central America's East Coast. At last report, the system 
was moving west, northwest over the Swan Islands at 13 km an hour. 
A hurricane warning was in effect for the islands and the entire coast 
of Honduras.

Good news on the job front for college seniors. A study released today 
by Michigan state University indicates a college degree plus technical 
or computer skills should equal a successful job search. Recruiters 
are hitting college campuses earlier to look for people to hire, and 
employers are hiring at about the same rate as last year. The school's 
28th annual recruiting trends survey found that engineering and computer 
science are the hottest fields.

Comedian flip Wilson has died. His assistant says Wilson died of liver 
cancer at his home in Malibu, california, Wednesday night. In the 
early 1970s, Wilson became the first successful African-American host 
of a TV variety show. "The flip Wilson show" ran on NBC from 1970 
to 1974. Wilson won two emmys for the show and earned national fame 
for his numerous sketch characters, including "Geraldine." Flip Wilson 
was 64.

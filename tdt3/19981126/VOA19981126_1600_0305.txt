Oil ministers from the Organization of Petroleum Exporting countries, 
OPEC, have left unchanged existing production curbs. OPEC ministers 
meeting in Vienna left in place a 10 percent cut in oil production 
agreed to in June. Despite the slump in world oil prices, the Ministers 
did not agree to cut production further.

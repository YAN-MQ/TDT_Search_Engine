Olympic Slalom Ski Champion Hilde Gerg of Germany has won her first 
career world cup downhill race. Gerg sped to victory in Friday's contest 
in Visane, Switzerland by four-one hundredths of a second over Sweden's 
Pernilla Wiberg. The Italian Viviana Perez provided the day's other 
surprise placing third. That was her first career top three finish 
in the downhill. World Cup Leader Alexandra Meissnitzer of Austria 
was fourth. She has won four races this season including two in Valdesair, 
France last week.

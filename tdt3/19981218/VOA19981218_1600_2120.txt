The United Nations says it plans to investigate the allegations of 
mass killings by opposition Afghan factions in Northern and Central 
Afghanistan. The world body says the investigation will be headed 
by its top human rights official Mary Robinson. The investigation 
will focus on allegations of mass killings of mainly civilians in 
the Afghan cities of Mazar-e Sharif and Baghlan in August of this 
year.

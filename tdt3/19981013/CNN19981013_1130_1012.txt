Monsanto has confirmed that its merger with American home products 
has been called off. The buyout of Monsanto had originally been valued 
at $34 billion. Pharmaceutical giant American home products makes 
a host of prescription and over-the-counter drugs, including Advil 
and robitussin. Monsanto is a chemical and biotechnology company. 
It produces everything from fertilizer to nutrasweet. Monsanto says 
the merger was not in the interest of shareholders of either company. 
This is one of the biggest mergers ever to be called off. Shares of 
Monsanto right now are losing 10 3/8. America home products is currently 
down 4.

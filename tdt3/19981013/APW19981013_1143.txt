A well-known Belarusian Orthodox priest was killed and his body dumped 
near a highway, the ITAR-Tass news agency reported Tuesday. A duty 
officer at the Belarusian national police headquarters confirmed that 
the dead body of Father Mikhail, secretary of the Brest-Kobrinsk diocese, 
was found Tuesday near a highway between the capital Minsk and the 
town of Brest, 300 kilometers (186 miles) to the west. He refused 
to give any more details. ITAR-Tass said that Father Mikhail's corpse 
bore signs of a violent attack, and that the police had launched an 
investigation.

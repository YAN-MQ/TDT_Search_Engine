The two new American Davis Cup players both won Tuesday at the Singapore 
event. Fourth seed Jan-Michael Gambill overpowered compatriot Jonathan 
Stark 6-1, 6-2. While Justin Gimelstob upset eighth seed Ramon Delgado 
of Paraguay 6-4, 6-4. Seventh seed Paul Haarhuis of The Netherlands 
was also an upset victim, falling to Kenneth Carlsen of Denmark in 
three sets. In a match between unseeded players, John van Lottum of 
The Netherlands beat Wayne Black of Zimbabwe.

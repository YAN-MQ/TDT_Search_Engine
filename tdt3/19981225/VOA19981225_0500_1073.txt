Only one game involving a rank team was played in U.S. College Basketball 
on Christmas Eve. Where the Washington Huskies handed 13th rank New 
Mexico its first loss of the season: 70 to 61. Deon Lutton tossed 
in 10 of his 22 points for Washington, in a decisive second half in 
Seattle. Todd McCullogh won the 21 points and 14 rebounds for the 
Huskies. Lutton scored eight straight points during an 18 to 6 run 
that put the game out of reach. Kenny Thomas scored a season high: 
26 points for New Mexico, which was playing on the road for the first 
time this season. The loss drops the Lobos to an eight and one record.

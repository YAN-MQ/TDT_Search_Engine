Thousands of people gathered at St. Peter's Basilica in Vatican City 
at midnight, Thursday. To listen to Pope John Paul II deliver his 
Christmas message. The Pope said prayers for world leaders and international 
organizations working for justice and peace. He prayed for exploited 
and mal-treated children and for all the world's sick. Especially 
those with terminal illnesses. The Pope also expressed his eagerness 
about Christmas celebrations next year. When Christianity enters its 
third millennium. He said he will inaugurate the event by opening 
the Basilica's usually closed doors.

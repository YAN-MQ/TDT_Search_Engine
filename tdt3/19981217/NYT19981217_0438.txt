The New York Times Co. said Thursday that Raymond Douglas had been 
promoted to corporate chief information officer and that Ellen Taus, 
the company treasurer, had received the additional title of vice president. 
Douglas had been vice president for systems and technology. Ms. Taus 
has been treasurer of the company since 1997. Douglas, who is 49, 
joined The Times in 1990 and is a graduate of Michigan State University 
and the Advanced Executive Program at the Newspaper Management Center 
of Northwestern University. Ms. Taus, who is 40, graduated from Northwestern 
University and has an MBA from Columbia University. She had previously 
worked in the corporate finance department at R.H. Macy &AMP; Co.

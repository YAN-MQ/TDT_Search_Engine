A small plane crashed Thursday afternoon into a residential neighborhood 
in this Central African capital city, killing the two pilots and damaging 
a number of houses, witnesses said. At least two other people were 
injured on the ground, the witnesses told state radio. Airport officials, 
speaking on condition of anonymity, said the plane belonged to the 
Gabonese local airline Air Service and was headed from Libreville 
to the coastal oil city Port Gentil. There were no passengers in the 
plane when it crashed between 4:30 p.m. and 5 p.m. (1530 GMT and 1600 
GMT), the officials said.

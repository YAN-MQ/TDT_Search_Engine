A seven-ton Christmas tree arrived in the big apple today. The 73-foot 
Norway spruce will be placed at Rockefeller center tomorrow. It will 
be decorated with over 26,000 light bulbs over the next three weeks. 
The tree from Richfield, Ohio, is the first Christmas tree from outside 
New York state to be placed at Rockefeller center. It is scheduled 
to be lit December 2nd.

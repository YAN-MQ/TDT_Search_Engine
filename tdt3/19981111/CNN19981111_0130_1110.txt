Dell computers is teaming up with Kodak to capitalize on the digital 
camera craze. Dell says it will sell Kodak digital cameras with some 
of its personal computers. Kodak predicts the move will boost its 
market share and increase profits. The companies are hoping to lure 
PC users who plan to send messages or photographs to family and friends 
this holiday season.

Ever wonder what happens to all those leftover pumpkins from halloween? 
In Washington, they get hauled to the national zoo. One African and 
three Asian elephants shared the feast Tuesday, digging into about 
8,000 pounds of pumpkin. This is the sixth year pumpkin farm operators 
have donated portions of their surplus crop to feed the pachyderms.

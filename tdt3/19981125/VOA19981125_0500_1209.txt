In U.S. college football, UCLA quarterback Cade McNown is the winner 
of the 1998 Johnny Unitas Golden Arm Award given annually to the nation's 
top senior quarterback. McNown guided UCLA to a perfect 10-and-0 record 
this season, putting the Bruins third in the national rankings. McNown 
passed for 2,617 yards and 18 touchdowns this season. He's thrown 
for a school record 9,855 yards and 61 touchdowns during his four-year 
career.

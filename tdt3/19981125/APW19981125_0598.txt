Prime Minister Jose Maria Aznar said Wednesday Spain will respect 
the ruling by British judges upholding Augusto Pinochet's arrest in 
London for possible extradition to Spain on charges of genocide. Aznar 
said his government, which opposed the human rights probe that led 
to Pinochet's arrest in London, would nonetheless show ``common sense 
and of course, strict observance of judicial independence.'' As live 
television coverage of the House of Lords' decision reverberated around 
Spain, hundreds of people, some crying from emotion, hugged and cheered 
at a rally in the Madrid's central Puerta del Sol plaza. (more)

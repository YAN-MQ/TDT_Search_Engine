President Leonid Kuchma appointed Deputy Prime Minister Mykola Biloblotsky 
as his new presidential chief of staff, Kuchma's press service said 
Wednesday. Biloblotsky, 55, an economist and former lawmaker, has 
served as labor and social policy minister since 1996 and later received 
the additional post of deputy prime minister.

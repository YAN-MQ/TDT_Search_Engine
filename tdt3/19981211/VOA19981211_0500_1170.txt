U.S. college football's leading candidate for the Heisman trophy, 
Ricky Williams of the University of Texas, was named the winner of 
the Doke Walker Award given to the nation's top running back. Williams 
also won it last year, the first player to win the award twice. Williams 
rushed for 2,124 yards and became college football's Division One 
all time leading rusher. The Heisman trophy will be presented on Saturday 
to college football's top player this season.

And a feasibility study has been completed for a project to build 
an industrial zone in Kontao province in Vietnam. Francis DeMez GMT 
Group assisted with the study. The 290-hectare Hao River industrial 
zone will be built at an estimated cost of some $54 million. It's 
planned to include a 60-hectare river port as well as a plant to process 
farm produce. Twenty French companies are said to have registered 
to lease land in the industrial zone.

A Thai airways jumbo jet with 146 people on board crashed in bad weather 
Friday evening while trying to land at a provincial airport in Southern 
Thailand. At least 44 people, including the pilot, survived the crash,

airport in Seratani. The plane burst into flames after it hit the 
ground. The jet was on a flight from Bangkok.

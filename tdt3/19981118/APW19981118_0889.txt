Kazimierz Zaklukiewicz, a leading Polish stage actor, has died of 
head injuries after being beaten by two teen-agers. Zaklukiewicz, 
47, was in the Lithuanian capital performing with a Polish theater 
group when he was attacked last week by two youths who beat him with 
a club. He died Tuesday night. The two youths arrested in connection 
with the attack will be charged with murder, prosecutors said. The 
attackers reportedly robbed Zaklukiewicz of the equivalent of dlrs 
30, the Baltic News Service reported.

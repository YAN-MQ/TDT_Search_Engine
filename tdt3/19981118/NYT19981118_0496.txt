Independent counsel Kenneth Starr is scheduled to appear before the 
House Judiciary Committee on Thursday starting at 10 a.m. ET. Five 
broadcasters _ CNN, the Fox News Channel, PBS, C-Span and MSNBC _ 
plan live gavel-to-gavel coverage. C-Span also plans to rebroadcast 
the day's hearing beginning at 7 p.m. ET. ABC, CBS and NBC will begin 
live coverage at 10 a.m. ET. Those networks say that the length of 
coverage has not been determined and that decisions will be made as 
the hearings progress.

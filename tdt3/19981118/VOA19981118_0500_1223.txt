The opposing sides in the National Basketball Association lockout 
met for the first time in 11 days, Tuesday and agreed to resume bargaining 
talks this Friday. It will be first meeting of the full owners and 
players negotiating committee since October 28. Both sides are still 
in disagreement over a framework for a deal and percentage of revenue 
owners will be devoting to salaries. The players are seeking 60 percent. 
The owners want 50 percent. All games for this month are canceled.

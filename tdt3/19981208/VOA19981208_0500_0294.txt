NATO foreign ministers are meeting in Brussels to redefine NATO's 
mission in response to new threats such as weapons of mass destruction. 
U.S. Secretary of State Madelaine Albright is expected to call for 
a NATO center designed to stop the spread of such weapons through 
increased sharing of military intelligence. In an article in Monday's 
Financial Times newspaper, Ms. Albright said that NATO must be prepared 
to handle threats outside its traditional territory of operations.

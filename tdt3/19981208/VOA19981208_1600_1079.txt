Well, it's time now for a look at sports. Here's VOA's Steve Schy. 
Well, for the second day in a row. A female Chinese weight-lifter 
has set a new world record. At the Asian Games in Bangkok, Thailand. 
On her way to winning the gold in the 53 kilogram class, Yang Zhia 
jerked 120 kilos. Breaking the record set by countrywoman Wang Ju 
Phem last month. Yang lifted a total of 212.5 kilograms. Which eclipsed 
Wang's mark of 210 kilos. China's Li Mao Shang won the gold in the 
men's 62 kilogram division. Meanwhile, China captured the team gold 
medal in the women's air pistol. Kuwait won the men's team skeet shooting 
event. With China second. Host country Thailand won the men's free 
rifle prone shooting event. And South Korea won two gold medals in 
Tae Kwon Do. While Mongolia won a gold medal in the women's lightweight 
division.

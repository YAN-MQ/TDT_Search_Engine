Paris is entering the crowded race to stage the 2008 Summer Olympics. 
French President Jacques Chirac telephoned International Olympic Committee 
President Juan Antonio Samuran Tuesday. To give him the news. An official 
announcement is expected to be made in the French capitol, Wednesday. 
There had been speculation that Paris would not submit a bid if China 
entered the race. And Beijing has indicated it want to bid for the 
Games. Beijing finished a close second to Sydney in the voting for 
the 2000 Olympics. Other cities which have also expressed interest 
for 2008 include Toronto, Buenos Aires, Istanbul, Osaka, Koala Lumpur, 
and Seville. Official bidding will be opened by the IOC next year.

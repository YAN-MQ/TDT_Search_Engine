Japan picked up another gold medal in mountain bike cycling with Akihito 
Udagawa taking the men's cross-country race Tuesday at the Asian Games. 
China's Ma Yanping beat Japan's Kanako Kobayashi in the women's race. 
Udagawa was timed in 2 hours, 5 minutes, 7 seconds, almost seven minutes 
ahead of Thailand's Surajit Jirowong. Third was China's Li Fuyu, another 
minute behind Surajit. Ma won in 2:07:28.00 to 2:11:45.00 for Kobayashi. 
Thailand's C. Singupatham won the bronze in 2:17:34.00. In Monday's 
opening cycling events, Japan's Sachiko Kamakura won the women's downhill 
race and Thailand's Phannarong Kongsamut took the men's.

Babies ruled at the box office over the weekend. Nickelodeon's "rugrats" 
was the top-grossing movie. It brought in an estimated $28 million.

starring will Smith and Gene Hackman. It opened in second place, bringing 
in just over $20 million. Adam Sandler's latest, "the waterboy," took 
the number three spot in its third week of release. The comedy brought 
in just under $16 million for the weekend.

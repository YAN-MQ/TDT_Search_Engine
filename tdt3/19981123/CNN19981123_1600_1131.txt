A transatlantic bank merger may be close at hand. Germany's Deutsche 
bank announcing today it is preparing to buy bankers trust. The $9.2 
billion deal still has to be finalized, but if it goes through, it 
would create one of the world's largest financial services companies. 
The deal also would be the largest takeover of a U.S. financial company 
by an overseas bank. Deutsche bank has long sought a foothold in the 
U.S. stock and bond underwriting business.

Japan won gold and silver and South Korea took the bronze Wednesday 
in the carom competition, a new cue sport in the Asian Games. Akio 
Shimada beat Ryuji Umeda 50-40 in the all-Japanese final. Kim Jung-gyu 
beat compatriot Kim Moo-soon 50-32 for the bronze. The new event is 
part of the billiards and snooker competition added to the games this 
year.

Iran edged Uzbekistan, 82-78, in men's basketball preliminary play 
Wednesday at the 13th Asian Games, with Mehran Hatami leading the 
Iranian offense with 19 points. A. Chernov scored 25 for the losers. 
The Philippines overwhelmed tiny Kyrgyzstan, 91-50, in the other men's 
contest. In women's preliminaries, China blew away the Philippines, 
116-40, while Kazakhstan defeated host nation Thailand, 79-67. Japan 
defeated Kyrgyzstan by default. (pm-tjh)

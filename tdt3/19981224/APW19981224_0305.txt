A U.S. marine was killed and three others injured when their vehicle 
rolled over during a military exercise in Kuwait this week, the U.S. 
military said Thursday. The marines were in a Humvee, a light desert 
jeep, when the accident occurred Monday, the military said in a statement. 
Lance Cpl. Lucas Williams was killed, said the statement issued by 
the 31st Marine Expeditionary Unit. The names of the injured were 
not released. The announcement of the accident was delayed until next 
of kin had been informed.

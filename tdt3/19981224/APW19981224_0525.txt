A Greek diplomat was shot and killed Thursday in the Georgian capital 
Tbilisi, police said. Anastasius Mizitrasos, 37, was a security attache 
at Greece's embassy in Tbilisi. He was found dead at the entrance 
to the house where he lived with his wife and child, said Demuri Beradze, 
duty officer of the district police in the prestigious Vake neighborhood 
of the capital. Mizitrasos had been in Tbilisi since March 1997. An 
investigation into the killing has been launched, Beradze said.

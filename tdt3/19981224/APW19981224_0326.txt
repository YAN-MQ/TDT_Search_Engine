Four people died of hypothermia in Moscow as temperatures remained 
well below freezing, a report said Thursday. The four people died 
Wednesday, bringing the toll to 65 so far this winter, the Interfax 
news agency reported. The temperature dipped to minus 18 Celsius (0 
Fahrenheit) on Thursday. Most of the victims are homeless people who 
either fall asleep or pass out from drinking.

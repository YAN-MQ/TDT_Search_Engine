Hungary's Foreign Minister Janos Martonyi arrived in Ukraine on Sunday 
for a two-day visit, a news report said. Martonyi's trip to Kiev is 
devoted to preparations for Ukrainian President Leonid Kuchma's visit 
to Hungary, scheduled for Oct. 27-28, the Interfax news agency reported. 
The Hungarian minister is to meet his Ukrainian counterpart Borys 
Tarasiuk later Sunday and on Monday Martonyi is to have meetings with 
Kuchma and Ukrainian Prime Minister Valery Pustovoitenko. In Hungary, 
Kuchma plans to discuss bilateral and international issues.

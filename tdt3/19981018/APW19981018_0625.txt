Britain's Prince Edward will pay a three-day visit to Jordan, beginning 
Nov. 27, the British Embassy said Sunday. Edward, the youngest son 
of Queen Elizabeth ll, will open an exhibition celebrating the 50th 
anniversary of British cultural center in Amman and the expansion 
of its language institute. The prince will be the guest of Jordan's 
Crown Prince Hassan, the embassy said in a statement.

Taiwan stopped Japan's ninth-inning rally at five runs Saturday and 
won 11-10 in Asian Games preliminary round baseball. After Japan took 
an early 5-1 lead, Taiwan (1-3) caught up with four runs in the fourth 
and went ahead 11-5 with six more over the next three innings. Chen 
Chih-yuan went 3-for-5 for Taiwan with a home run and four runs batted 
in. For Japan (1-2), Yoshihiko Kajiyama went 3-for-4 with one home 
run and five RBIs. Shinnosuke Abe also homered. Earlier, China (3-0) 
drubbed the Philippines (1-3) 11-1. South Korea (3-0) and Thailand 
(1-2) were idle Saturday.

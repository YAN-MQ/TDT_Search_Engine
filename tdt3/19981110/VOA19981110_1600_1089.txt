Thank you very much, Victor. France football magazine has included 
seven members of European champion Rayal Madrid to its list of candidates 
for the Golden Ball World Player of the Year Award. Rayal's team members 
on the list includes Spain's Fernando Ero and Raoul Gonzales, Croatia's 
Robert Yarney and Dabar Sooker, and Yugoslav Praydac Miyatavich. Also 
on the list are Roberto Carlos of Brazil and Clarence Sedorf of the 
Netherlands. English club champion Arsenal and Italian champion Yuventes 
each have five players on the list. Iyac's Amsterdam and Inter Milan 
Club each have four players nominated. The French magazine will poll 
soccer journalists from around Europe for the award. The winner will 
be announced December 22nd. Last year, Brazil's Reynaldo easily won 
the prize ahead of Miyatavich and French player, Zinidin Zodam.

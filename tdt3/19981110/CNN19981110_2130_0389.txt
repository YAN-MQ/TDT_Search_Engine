Yoga is better than wrist braces for people with carpal tunnel syndrome. 
Chiropractic manipulation won't relieve tension headaches. And Chinese 
herbs can give relief to those with irritable bowel syndrome. Those

published by the "journal of the American medical association." Americans 
spent $27 billion on such treatments last year. While some work and 
some don't, the "journal's" editor cautions against blindly accepting 
untested and unproven alternative therapies.

Officials in St. Joseph, Missouri say three people are dead and at 
least two others are wounded after a gunman began shooting randomly 
near the center of town. A police spokesman said police shot and killed 
the gunman after cornering him behind a church. A police officer and 
a firefighter were also reported among the dead. Witnesses said the 
gunman began firing from the window of an apartment building then 
walked out onto the street and kept firing.

Ukraine's trade deficit totaled dlrs 1.7 billion over the first nine 
months of this year, jumping by dlrs 230 million in August alone, 
the State Statistics Committee said Tuesday. Exports from the former 
Soviet republic totaled dlrs 8.99 billion from January to September 
of this year, and imports stood at dlrs 10.69 billion, it said. Despite 
the economic crisis in Russia, the country remains Ukraine's largest 
trading partner, accounting for 48 percent of its imports and 24 percent 
of its exports.

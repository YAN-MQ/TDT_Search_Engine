Gunpowder exploded at a defense plant in Siberia, causing extensive 
damage but causing no injuries, a news report said. The powerful blast 
in the Ural Mountains city of Perm took place late Wednesday night

plant was located in a forest area and did not threaten any other 
buildings, the report added.

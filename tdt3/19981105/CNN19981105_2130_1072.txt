Three international airlines have decided to take steps to decrease 
the number of incidents of unruly behavior by passengers. Air France 
estimates the number of incidents has doubled in the past year alone. 
So along with British airways and virgin atlantic, it plans to take 
tougher legal action when passengers become violent, abusive or refuse 
to obey nonsmoking regulations. In addition, the airlines will improve 
training of flight personnel to handle difficult passengers.

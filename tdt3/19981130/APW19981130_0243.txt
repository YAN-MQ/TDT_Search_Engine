Two Ukrainian teenagers were killed by an explosion at a former army 
training ground in the southern city of Kryvyi Rig, officials said 
Monday. The youths, aged 16 and 18, were apparently trying Sunday 
to take apart a shell or some other kind of ammunition, said the Emergency 
Situations Ministry. Authorities launched an investigation into the 
incident, it said.

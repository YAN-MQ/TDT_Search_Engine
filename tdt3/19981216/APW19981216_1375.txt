The body of a missing reporter for the San Antonio Express-News was 
found Wednesday in a ravine in the western part of the country, a 
U.S. consular official said. Philip True apparently slipped and fell 
to his death into a deep ravine in a remote mountain range on the 
border between Jalisco and Nayarit states, a spokesperson at the Guadalajara 
consulate said. The spokesperson spoke on customary condition of anonymity.

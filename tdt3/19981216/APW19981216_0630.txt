South Korea's handball team defeated Kuwait 29-18 in the men's final 
Wednesday to capture an Asian Games gold medal. Korea held off Kuwait 
15-12 in the first half and widened the gap in the second with 14 
goals to Kuwait's 6. Japan defeated Iran 28-23 for the bronze after 
leading 16-12 at halftime. The Korean team had finished atop Pool 
B, followed by Iran, China and Qatar in the preliminary round. Silver 
medalist Kuwait led Pool A, followed by Japan, United Arab Emirates 
and Thailand.

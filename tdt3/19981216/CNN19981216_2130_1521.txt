Astronaut and senator John Glenn says of all the awards he has received 
since returning from space, nothing can compare with coming home. 
Glenn was honored with a parade through the streets of Columbus, Ohio, 
today. He and his wife annie road aboard a float with a space shuttle 
replica. Glenn later spoke to a joint session of the house and Senate 
at the statehouse. In October, at age 77, Glenn became the oldest 
person to travel in space.

Defending champion Italy and four other favorites won their second 
straight preliminary-round victories Saturday, securing berths in 
the second round of the Men's World Volleyball Championship. The four 
teams were Atlanta Olympic gold medalist Netherlands, Olympic bronze 
medalist Yugoslavia, this year's World League champion Cuba and Russia. 
Also winning for a clean 2-0 record were Spain, the United States, 
China, Argentina, Brazil, Bulgaria and Yugoslavia. The top 16 teams 
from a field of 24 will advance to the second round starting next 
Wednesday for a spot in the 12-team final round Nov. 28-29 in Tokyo.
